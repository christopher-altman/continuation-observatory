Method / limits

Structural measurement with explicit limits.

UCIP uses a three-part structure: identify what to test, measure the structural response, and define what would disprove it.

Problem

Behavioral self-report is easy to elicit and hard to interpret.

Approach

UCIP looks for differences in trajectory-derived latent organization under continuation-sensitive conditions.

Limits

Current signals are measurable and falsifiable, but they do not establish consciousness or moral status.
